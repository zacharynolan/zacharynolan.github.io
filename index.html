Welcome! I am an assistant professor of marketing at the University of Arizona's Eller College of Management.

My research applies methods from econometrics, statistics, and machine learning to study topics at the intersection of economics and marketing. My recent work explores the pricing of internet access, consumer behavior online, and the economics of digital platforms.

I received my PhD in economics from Duke University. Prior to that, I received a BS in mathematics and a BA in economics from the University of Florida in my hometown of Gainesville.
