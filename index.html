I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Economics at Duke University. My research interests include empirical industrial organization, applied microeconomics, and telecommunications.

My dissertation research estimates demand for online video content and considers the implications of internet pricing for competition, firm profitability, and social surplus.
